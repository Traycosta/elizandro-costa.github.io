Projects

Onikura

Onikura is a 2D Boss-Rush platformer game, focused on giving the player the freedom to play and progress in their own style, offering various options to upgrade their character and unique abilities that completely change their playstyle. With unique scenarios and soundtracks, the game offers challenging combat and boss fights that will stay with you forever.

My contribution: I was responsible for the level design of the 3 main levels, the design and balancing of player mechanics, and the creation of the GDD (Game Design Document).

Unity C# Level Design GDD

Gem Fusion Puzzle

A prototype of a match-3 game developed on the Upit.AI platform as a sample for mobile games using AI.

My contribution: I developed the initial concept, the game system design, and created the prototype of the main gameplay loop (matching identical pieces and reaching the maximum score without losing all the stars).

Upit.AI Rapid Prototyping System Design

Casual Hybrid Game Design Study

A personal project focused on analyzing casual hybrid games and the "match-3 + meta-progression" level design of Royal Match!, using Unity for prototyping.

My contribution: Player flow analysis, Higher Retention than hyper-casual (players stay longer), Better Monetization than pure hyper-casual (IAP + ads), and documentation of my design conclusions.

Unity Engine Level Design Analysis Rapid Prototyping

About Me

Hello! I'm a motivated and creative Game Designer.

Since childhood, video games have been my greatest passion. Not just playing them, but taking them apart in my head to understand how they work: why is a mechanic fun? What makes a level memorable? What makes a story resonate with the player?

This curiosity led me to study Game Design, where I could turn my passion into a skill. I love working in a team, solving complex problems, and rapidly prototyping ideas to see if they are fun in practice.

I am always learning new tools and studying industry trends. Right now, I am looking for an opportunity as a Junior Game Designer to contribute with my energy and creativity, and to grow professionally in a stimulating environment.
